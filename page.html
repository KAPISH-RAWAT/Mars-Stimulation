Martian Soil

Martian soil is a dry,dusty mix of sand amd minerals rich in iron oxide,giving Mars its red color.It lacks organic material but contains frozen water in some areas,making it challenging for plant growth yet valuable for future exploration.

Martian Atmosphere

The Martian atmosphere is very thin and mostly made of carbon dioxide,offering little protection or warmth.It causes big temperature changes and frequent dust storms that can cover large areas of the planet.

Rover

We have facilate Mars with Rover that are used in research and surveillance.It is very helpful to explore the places on Mars where it is hard for humans to reach.

Drone

We have facilate Mars with Drones that are used in research and surveillance.It is very helpful to explore the places on Mars where it is hard for humans to research.

Oxygenator

Oxygenator is the device that convert carbon dioxide into oxygen. It is very essential in our mission to make life possible on Mars.It also produces carbon mono oxide as product which is used as fuel.

Central Pyramid

The central pyramid is the place,where all the data of mars research is stored.It also display the temperature and humidity level.It's the heart of the Martian Colony handling all major tasks.

Shelter House

Martian colony is the place where all the human resides on mars.It has all the facilities from comfortable resting place to water and light facilities.

Green House

Green house is the place where flora and fauna from Earth is preserved.It also serve the purpose of growing food.It is beneficial in making a plant species that can be grow on martian surface.

Storm Predictor

Storms are very common on the surface of mars.Therefore there is the need to develop the storm predictor that help us protect ourselves from these deadly storms.It produce alarming sound when storm in approaching.
